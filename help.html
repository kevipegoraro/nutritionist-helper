How to Use

NavigateUse the left menu to move between proposal, design, info, anamnese, TMB, and help pages.
Fill dataEnter the patient data in the Anamnese page. The top bar and calculator update automatically.
Review calculationsOpen TMB / GET to check the calculated metabolic values and adjust activity factor or planned deficit/superávit.
ExportUse “Salvar .txt” to download a text summary or “Imprimir / Salvar PDF” to use the browser print dialog.
